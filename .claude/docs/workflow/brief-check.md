# Brief Check

**Purpose:** Validate current findings against the problem statement and success signals. A mid-phase sanity check.

---

## Steps

1. Read the current brief.
2. Re-read the **Problem** and **Success Signal** sections.
3. Read the **Findings** section of the current phase.
4. Ask:
   - Do the findings address the problem as stated?
   - Do the findings cover all success signal items, or identify a path to covering them?
   - Is anything missing -- a success signal with no findings, or a finding that doesn't map to any signal?
   - Has the research revealed that the problem statement itself needs updating?
5. Report gaps and suggest next steps.
